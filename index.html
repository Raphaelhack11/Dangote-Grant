About The Campaign

Dedicated to Justice
Across America

Founded by Collin Myers, this campaign was born from a deep commitment to protecting ordinary Americans from the rapidly growing threat of financial fraud, online scams, and organized criminal networks. Our mission is simple: no victim should face these crimes alone.

Working in close cooperation with the FBI's Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3), our team investigates reported cases, coordinates victim advocacy, and pursues every available legal avenue to recover stolen funds and hold perpetrators accountable.

Active Campaigns

Our Priority Focus Areas

Six active programs targeting the most prevalent forms of financial crime and fraud affecting Americans today.

💸
Scam Funds Recovery
Our flagship initiative dedicated to recovering money lost to online scammers, romance fraudsters, investment schemers, and cryptocurrency theft rings operating across the U.S.
→
🌐
Cyber Fraud Takedown
Targeting phishing operations, identity theft networks, and digital fraud syndicates preying on American consumers, seniors, and small business owners.
→
👴
Elder Protection Program
Specialized advocacy and rapid-response support for seniors targeted by grandparent scams, Social Security fraud, and predatory telephone schemes.
→
🏘️
Community Safety Initiative
Grassroots education and awareness programs deployed across all 50 states, equipping citizens with tools to identify and avoid criminal targeting.
→
💳
Banking & Wire Fraud Unit
Working alongside financial institutions to trace, freeze, and recover funds moved via wire transfer, ACH fraud, and unauthorized account access.
→
📱
Social Media Crime Bureau
Investigating fraud originating on social platforms — fake profiles, romance scams, impersonation schemes, and social engineering attacks.
